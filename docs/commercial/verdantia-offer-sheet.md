## Positioning

Verdantia helps small and mid-sized teams move from scattered AI experiments to safe, practical, repeatable AI workflows.

**Short version:** Practical AI training and adoption support for teams.

**Founder/company frame:** Verdantia is the commercial vehicle. Gbemi Akadiri is the visible trust engine: trainer, speaker, practitioner, and adoption guide.

## Who this is for

- **SMEs and smaller corporate teams** that have started using AI but lack shared practice, review habits, and workflow structure.
- **Training providers** that need practical GenAI delivery capacity or specialist AI sessions for clients and learners.
- **Nonprofits and community organisations** that need accessible AI literacy and practical workflow support.

## What Verdantia does not lead with

- Generic “AI automation agency” positioning.
- Vague “AI transformation” claims.
- Custom assistants before the use case, workflow, data, and risk picture are clear.
- Fake scale, invented case studies, or over-polished consultancy theatre.

## Proof posture

Until stronger client proof exists, lead with founder-led honesty:

> This is practical, scoped AI training and adoption support. The value is clarity, usable material, safe working habits, and a route your team can actually follow.

## Soft CTA

If your team is exploring AI but not sure how to turn experiments into useful practice, Verdantia can help you choose a sensible first step.

Start with: **AI Team Briefing** or **Practical AI Workshop** unless the team already has clear use cases and urgency.
