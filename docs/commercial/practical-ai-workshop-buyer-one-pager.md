## Practical AI training for teams that need safer, better workplace habits

Most teams are already experimenting with AI. The problem is that the use is scattered: one person uses ChatGPT for writing, another tries Copilot, someone else may paste sensitive information into a tool they do not fully understand, and managers are left wondering what is useful, what is risky, and what should become normal practice.

Verdantia helps teams turn AI experimentation into practical, safer, repeatable ways of working.

It works well for:

## What the workshop covers

Participants learn how to:

1. Understand where AI tools help and where they mislead.
2. Match AI tools to real workplace tasks.
3. Write better prompts using role, task, context, constraints, output format, and review criteria.
4. Review AI output for accuracy, privacy, tone, bias, and usefulness.
5. Use synthetic, anonymised, or organisation-approved examples only during exercises.
6. Identify practical use cases worth testing inside the team.
7. Build safer working habits around human review and responsible use.

## What this workshop is not

This is not a hype talk, a parade of AI tools, or a promise that AI will automate everyone’s work. It is a practical working session that helps your team understand where AI can help, where it can mislead, and how to use it with better judgement, safer prompts, and human review.

## FAQ

**Do participants need paid AI tools?**
No. The workshop can use the tools your organisation already approves. If live tool use is restricted, Verdantia can teach from prepared examples, screenshots, and safe demonstration material.

**Is this Microsoft Copilot-specific?**
It can be, but it does not have to be. The focus is better workplace use across approved tools and the judgement needed to use them safely.

**Can this be tailored to our team?**
Yes. The standard workshop includes light tailoring through a scoping call. Deeper tailoring, sector-specific examples, or custom materials can be scoped separately.

**How is sensitive data handled?**
Participants are not asked to paste sensitive client, employee, financial, or confidential information into public AI tools. Exercises use synthetic, anonymised, or approved examples.

**What happens after the workshop?**
The team receives reusable materials and recommended next steps. If useful, Verdantia can provide a follow-on use-case debrief, an Adoption Day, or a focused Adoption Sprint.
