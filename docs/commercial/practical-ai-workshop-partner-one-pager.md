## Practical GenAI and Copilot training delivery capacity

Verdantia is a founder-led AI training and adoption practice led by Gbemi Akadiri. The focus is practical workplace AI: helping teams use tools like Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity with better judgement, safer habits, and clearer use cases.

For training providers, consultancies, and partner organisations, Verdantia can support GenAI programmes as a specialist delivery partner or co-delivery resource.

## Partner fit

Verdantia is useful when a partner needs:

- Practical GenAI or Microsoft Copilot workshop delivery.
- A facilitator who can explain AI clearly to non-technical and mixed-skill groups.
- Workplace examples rather than abstract AI theory.
- Responsible-use guidance around privacy, review, hallucinations, and safe adoption.
- Flexible delivery using partner materials only with written permission and agreed usage boundaries, Verdantia materials, or an explicitly agreed blended approach.

## Delivery modes

1. **Co-delivery**
   - Verdantia supports an existing partner programme.
   - Useful when the partner owns the client relationship and needs AI-training capacity.

2. **Specialist module delivery**
   - Gbemi delivers a GenAI/Copilot module inside a wider training or transformation programme.

3. **Verdantia workshop delivery**
   - Verdantia delivers its own Practical AI Workshop format under agreed commercial terms.

4. **Custom adaptation**
   - Materials are adapted for a client sector, role group, or approved toolset only within agreed confidentiality, IP, and data-use boundaries.

## Partner terms snapshot

- **Delivery models:** partner-branded, co-branded, or Verdantia-branded delivery.
- **Typical formats:** 60–90 minute briefing, half-day workshop, full-day adoption session, or specialist module inside a wider programme.
- **Lead time:** ideally 10 business days for light tailoring; longer for custom sector material.
- **Materials:** Verdantia retains its core curriculum and delivery IP. Partner/client-specific adaptations can be agreed separately.
- **Commercials:** agreed per engagement, with delivery format, preparation, branding, materials, and customisation scoped separately.
- **Reliability:** sessions use Verdantia’s delivery reliability protocol: offline deck, prepared examples, backup demos, tech check, and framework-first fallback.


## Core topics

- AI tool literacy: Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity.
- Prompting for real work: role, task, context, constraints, output format, review criteria.
- Safe use: privacy, sensitive data, hallucinations, verification, tone, bias, accountability.
- Role-based exercises: admin, operations, sales/marketing, management, training, support, nonprofit/community teams.
- Use-case discovery: value, risk, ease, ownership, next steps.
- Adoption habits: moving from scattered experiments to repeatable team practice.

## Delivery experience

Gbemi has delivered Microsoft Copilot and introductory AI training for public-sector, financial-services, and workplace teams. Some delivery has taken place in partner/co-delivery contexts. Client and partner names are not listed unless explicitly approved in writing.

## Delivery reliability

Verdantia uses a delivery reliability protocol for live sessions:

- PDF/offline deck.
- Preloaded tabs.
- Screenshots or video backups for demos.
- Tech check 30–60 minutes before delivery.
- Framework-first fallback if tools or connectivity fail.

## Partner conversation starter

A useful first conversation is to identify the learner audience, the client promise, the delivery format, whether materials already exist, and whether Verdantia is needed as co-delivery, specialist module delivery, or full workshop delivery.

> Where are your clients asking about AI or Copilot, and what kind of delivery capacity or practical module would help you respond without overbuilding a full programme?
